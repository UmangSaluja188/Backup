YOGA GUIDELINES

1. Safety is more important when we doing the poses. Do not overstretch the muscles but gradually work your way into more advaced way.

2. If you experience more pain ,loss of range of motion, swelling, chest pain, inability to bear full body weight and shortness of breath do not continue.

3. Keep your mind clear when doing the poses. Try not to think about what you have to do tommorrow.

4. Do yoga in a dim light area and play light music to help with your concentration.

5. When performing postures keep the breath flow slow, steady and even.

6. Hold the poses for 510 breaths. Breath deep into the diaphragm and out the nose.

7. Yoga is a form of exercise so best to do on an empty stomach or wait 1.52 hours after your meal.

8. If a pose does not feel right do not hold it but look for variations. It does not have to be picture perfect all tha time.
